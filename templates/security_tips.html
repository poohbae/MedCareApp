Security Tips

Follow these best practices to keep your account and data secure:

  • Use a strong password with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  • Never share your login credentials with anyone.
  • Avoid logging in over public Wi-Fi or shared networks.
  • Always log out after using the system, especially on shared computers.
  • Verify system emails or messages to avoid phishing attempts.
  • Keep your browser and device software updated.
  • Do not disable browser security warnings or cookie protections.
